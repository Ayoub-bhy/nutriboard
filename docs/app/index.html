⌚ Connected Devices sync activity to sharpen your targets

Connect a wearable or health platform and NutriBoard pulls in your steps, active calories, sleep and heart rate — then adjusts your daily budget so you can eat back what you burn.

Live sync uses each provider's OAuth API (Fitbit, Garmin, Oura, Whoop, Strava, Withings) or Google Health Connect; Apple Watch syncs through Apple Health. A unified API such as Terra or the open-source Open Wearables can connect 500+ devices at once — enabled on the hosted version.
