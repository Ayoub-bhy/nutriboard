Your built-in coach

A coach who reads your day.

It sees your logs, targets, allergies and local weather, confirms your details first, then answers with specific amounts — raw to buy and cooked to eat. It's a rule-based coach (not a dietitian); add your own key for live AI.

  • ✓ Keeps foods in your allergies out of every suggestion
  • ✓ Gives raw (to buy) and cooked (to eat) grams
