💬 Your Nutrition Coach built-in coach

What this coach is: a simple rule-based assistant (keyword matching), not a live AI and not a dietitian. It reads your logs and profile, keeps foods in your allergies/dislikes out of suggestions, and gives raw (to buy) and cooked (to eat) grams. Its food numbers are approximate reference values, not the official CIQUAL dataset. For a real AI reply, add your own Anthropic API key on the right.

⌚ Connected Devices sync activity to sharpen your targets

Note: in this free browser-only version these provider buttons are a demo — they don't actually connect Fitbit/Garmin/etc. Real OAuth device sync runs on the hosted backend (not yet deployed). What does work right now is live phone/laptop sensors below (Bluetooth heart-rate, motion step counter, GPS walk) and manually entering today's activity.

Live sync uses each provider's OAuth API (Fitbit, Garmin, Oura, Whoop, Strava, Withings) or Google Health Connect; Apple Watch syncs through Apple Health. A unified API such as Terra or the open-source Open Wearables can connect 500+ devices at once — enabled on the hosted version.

📏 Measurement units

Pick how body weight, height and water are shown. Metric uses kilograms, centimetres and millilitres. Imperial uses pounds, inches and fluid ounces. Food gram amounts always follow the Ciqual database.

🔔 Smart reminders

NutriBoard can send you friendly notifications on this device — your phone or laptop — to drink water, move your body, take your vitamins, and check today's meal suggestions. Turn on notifications first, then choose what you want to be reminded about.

—
💧 Drink water
A gentle nudge every couple of hours while you are awake until you reach your daily water goal.
🚶 Move & walk
A reminder in the afternoon to get up, stretch and take a short walk to reach your step goal.
💊 Take vitamins
A daily reminder at the time you choose to take any vitamins or supplements with food.
🍳 Meal suggestions
Around your meal times, a reminder to open Suggested Meals and pick a Ciqual-based meal that fits your remaining budget.

🔒 Privacy & your data

Your data is stored privately in this browser, separated per person. You are always in control: download a full copy whenever you want, or permanently erase everything for this profile on this device. Nothing is sold or shared, and there are no ads.

Food calories and macros are representative entries from the ANSES-CIQUAL database and may differ slightly from a specific brand or recipe. NutriBoard gives educational estimates, not medical advice.

Honesty note: calorie targets use the Mifflin-St Jeor equation — an estimate, not a measurement. Food calories and macros are approximate per-100 g reference values compiled from common food-composition data; they are NOT the official ANSES-CIQUAL dataset and can differ from a specific brand, recipe or the exact CIQUAL entry — check ciqual.fr or the product label for anything that matters. NutriBoard is an educational tool, not medical or dietetic advice. Your data is stored only in this browser (nothing is sent to a server), separated per account or guest.
